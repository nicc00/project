What is your website about?

I plan to create a collection of my favorite movies. It will be somewhat like a blog where I post information on some movies that I recommend.

What is the objective of your website? (Also read as why would this site be important?)

I hope this website will help people who are interested in watching movies but don't know where to start. I want to provide my own personal recommenations.

Who is your target audience set?

My target audience are people who are interested in movies but don't watch them very often.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

I will include the movie with ways to watch, various ratings, images and perhaps trailers as well. I don't want to spoil movies by providing in-depth reviews so I will refrain from that.
